Our Story · Belagavi, India

Rooted in Nature,
Driven by Science

Alora was founded with a passion for food longevity — preserving nature's peak nutrition through advanced dehydration technology, supporting local farmers, and delivering wholesome ingredients to the world.

How It Started

From Belagavi to the World

Alora Dehydrated Fruits was born in the heart of Karnataka, India — a region blessed with rich agricultural heritage and access to some of the finest natural produce in the country.

We started with a vision rooted in food longevity — to preserve nature's nutrition at its peak, reducing waste while delivering wholesome ingredients to global buyers.

Using advanced freeze dehydration and mechanical dehydration technology, we lock in the natural vitamins, minerals, and goodness of every fruit and vegetable — exactly as nature intended.

Our Technology

Advanced Dehydration Technology

We use two state-of-the-art preservation methods — no sun drying, no contamination, no compromise on quality or nutrition.

❄️

Freeze Dehydration

Freeze drying removes moisture at ultra-low temperatures, preserving up to 98% of the original nutrients, colour, and flavour. The gold standard in food preservation — trusted by pharmaceutical and premium nutraceutical industries worldwide.

Up to 98% Nutrient Retention
⚙️

Mechanical Dehydration

Precision-controlled mechanical dehydration removes moisture in a fully hygienic, temperature-regulated environment. No sun exposure, no contamination risk — consistent quality in every single batch.

Hygienic · Controlled · Consistent
