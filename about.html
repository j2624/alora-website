Our Beginning
What Led Us Here

Every year, millions of tonnes of fresh fruits and vegetables are wasted across India — not because they are bad, but because they don't reach the right hands in time.

We started Alora with a simple but powerful solution — dehydration.

By gently removing moisture from organic, farm fresh produce sourced directly from local farmers, we extend the shelf life naturally — from days to up to 12 months through mechanical dehydration, and up to 25 years through freeze dehydration — without adding a single preservative.

"Less waste. More nutrition. Pure, honest food that travels further and lasts longer."

Sourcing & Farming
How We Grow — Three Methods

We believe quality begins long before processing. It starts with how ingredients are grown — the farming method, the soil, the care. We work with three distinct farming approaches.

🌱

Organic Farming

Certified Natural

Grown without synthetic pesticides, herbicides, or chemical fertilisers. Natural composting, crop rotation, and traditional methods maintain healthy soil and biodiversity.

  • No synthetic chemicals or pesticides
  • Natural composting and crop rotation
  • Soil health preserved for future generations
  • Higher antioxidant and nutrient density
  • Supports biodiversity and ecosystem balance
💧

Hydroponic Farming

Soil-Free · Precision Grown

We grow our own fresh herbs in nutrient-rich water — no soil, no pesticides. Precision farming means consistent, clean, high-quality produce year-round regardless of season.

  • Up to 90% less water than traditional farming
  • No soil-borne diseases or pests
  • Precise nutrient delivery to each plant
  • Year-round production, any climate
  • Faster growth cycles, consistent quality
🌾

Traditional Farming

Farm-Fresh · Seasonal

Strong partnerships with traditional farming communities across Karnataka, Maharashtra, and Kerala — seasonal crops harvested at peak ripeness for maximum flavour and nutrition.

  • Harvested at peak ripeness for best flavour
  • Direct farmer relationships and fair pricing
  • Seasonal produce — naturally at its best
  • Supports local farming livelihoods
  • Fully traceable from field to finished product
