Fresh Mixed Fruits Bowl Fresh Tomatoes Fresh Bananas Fresh Vegetables and Herbs Organic · Farm Fresh · Naturally Grown · No Preservatives Added Our Beginning What Led Us Here Every year, millions of tonnes of fresh fruits and vegetables are wasted across India — not because they are bad, but because they don't reach the right hands in time. We started Alora with a simple but powerful solution — dehydration. By gently removing moisture from organic, farm fresh, naturally grown produce sourced directly from local farmers in Karnataka and Maharashtra, we extend the shelf life naturally — from days to up to 12 months through mechanical dehydration, and up to 25 years through freeze dehydration — without adding a single preservative. The result? Less waste. More nutrition. Pure, honest food that travels further and lasts longer — while still carrying the goodness of the farm it came from. 🌾 Food Waste Problem Millions of tonnes of fresh produce wasted annually across India — we are part of the solution. 💧 Dehydration Solution Mechanical dehydration extends shelf life to 12 months.
