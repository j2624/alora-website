Our Beginning
What Led Us Here

Every year, millions of tonnes of fresh fruits and vegetables are wasted across India — not because they are bad, but because they don't reach the right hands in time.

We started Alora with a simple but powerful solution — dehydration.

By gently removing moisture from organic, farm fresh, naturally grown produce sourced directly from local farmers in Karnataka and Maharashtra, we extend the shelf life naturally — from days to up to 12 months through mechanical dehydration, and up to 25 years through freeze dehydration — without adding a single preservative.

The result? Less waste. More nutrition. Pure, honest food that travels further and lasts longer — while still carrying the goodness of the farm it came from.

How It Started
From Belagavi to the World

Alora Customary Care began with a simple belief — that pure, honest food can change lives.

What started as one woman's dream in Belagavi is now becoming a reality — a food brand built on courage, learning, and a deep passion for natural ingredients.

Founded with a clear mission to deliver high-quality dehydrated food products that meet global standards, Alora was born from a desire to preserve nature's goodness — reducing waste, supporting local farmers, and bringing wholesome ingredients to buyers worldwide.

"Every blend, every powder, every product is crafted with care. We don't rush the process — we respect it."

From a vision rooted in Belagavi's rich agricultural heritage, Alora stands for purity, trust, and the determination to build something truly meaningful. This journey is just beginning — and we invite you to grow with us.

"What began as a small home-based initiative is growing into a modern, process-driven food brand focused on consistency, hygiene, and natural ingredients."

We work in small batches to maintain freshness, follow clean processing practices, and ensure every product reflects our commitment to quality.


Our produce is naturally grown — chemical-free, farm fresh, and handled with care from harvest to packaging. We use the word "naturally grown" with pride — because our farmers grow with care, not chemicals.
