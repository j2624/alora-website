An Open Approach to Innovation

To us, innovation isn't confined to a lab. Every new Alora product starts as a simple question — a family recipe, a request from a UK buyer, a gap we spot in the market. Ideas can come from anywhere: our kitchen in Belagavi, feedback from tutoring students, or a customer's WhatsApp message.

Innovation isn't just about the product itself — it's about the packaging, the processing, and how a product actually reaches your table. We stay close to every part of that journey, so every new addition to the Alora range is thought through end to end.

Our Home Kitchen, Where It Begins

Every Alora recipe is first tested in a real kitchen, not a factory line. Before any product reaches production, it's tasted, adjusted and tasted again — the same way you'd perfect a family recipe.

We work directly with our dehydration and packaging partners in Belagavi to make sure what works in the kitchen translates faithfully into every pack we send out — no shortcuts, no compromise on taste.

Planning & Approval
Before Any Product Goes to Market

Every product moves through the same careful checkpoints before it earns a place in the Alora range.

Product Development

↗

Once an idea is agreed, we move to developing it for real — sourcing raw materials responsibly, testing dehydration methods, and benchmarking against similar products already on the market before anything is approved for the next stage.

Scale Up

↗

With the recipe confirmed, we move from small kitchen batches to full production quantities — checking consistency, shelf life and packaging integrity at every step before committing to a full run.

Launch

↗

The finished product is listed on the Alora website and shared with our UK and India customers — with full documentation, FSSAI compliance and Udyam registration in place before it ever reaches a shelf.

Exceptional Standards of Food Safety & Quality

As a growing food business, we take food safety seriously at every stage. From MSME/Udyam registration to FSSAI compliance in progress, every Alora product is made with hygiene, traceability and honesty at its core.
