Aloras Food Recipes

Simple, colourful, global-friendly recipes using Aloras natural dehydrated ingredients.

Golden Turmeric Latte

A warm, soothing drink made with Aloras Turmeric Powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk (250 ml)
  • 1/2 tsp Aloras Turmeric Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Aloras Ginger Powder
  • 1 tsp honey or jaggery

Method

  1. Warm the milk on low heat.
  2. Add turmeric and ginger powder.
  3. Stir well and simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Sweeten and serve hot.

Instant Ginger Tea

A quick, refreshing tea using Aloras Ginger Powder.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1/2 tsp Aloras Ginger Powder
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Honey to taste

Method

  1. Add ginger powder to hot water.
  2. Mix well and add lemon juice.
  3. Sweeten and enjoy.

Banana Powder Smoothie

A creamy, nutritious smoothie perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk or plant milk
  • 2 tbsp Aloras Banana Powder
  • 1 tsp honey
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Method

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth.
  3. Serve chilled.
