Indian

Amla Pickle

With Aloras Amla Powder

Tangy, spicy, perfect with rice or roti.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Amla Powder
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp chilli powder
  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

Method

  1. Heat oil and crackle mustard seeds.
  2. Turn off flame, add powders.
  3. Mix and store.
