How to Use Alora Products

Vitamin C Orange Peel Tea

A warming citrus tea using Alora Orange Peel Powder — an easy daily immunity boost. 5 mins, 1 Cup.

Ingredients: Hot water 1 cup, Alora Orange Peel Powder ½ tsp, Honey 1 tsp, Lemon juice few drops.

Method: Add Alora Orange Peel Powder to hot water. Stir in honey and lemon juice. Mix well and enjoy warm.

Pomegranate Power Bowl

A vibrant, antioxidant-rich breakfast bowl with Alora Pomegranate Powder stirred through creamy yogurt. 10 mins, 1 Bowl.

Ingredients: Thick yogurt 1 cup, Alora Pomegranate Powder 1 tsp, Granola 3 tbsp, Honey 1 tsp, Fresh pomegranate arils for topping.

Method: Stir Pomegranate Powder into yogurt until swirled pink. Spoon into a bowl. Top with granola, honey and arils. Serve chilled.

Beetroot Hummus

A vivid pink, creamy hummus with the earthy sweetness of Alora Beetroot Powder — great with warm pita. 10 mins, Serves 4.

Ingredients: Chickpeas (boiled) 1 cup, Alora Beetroot Powder 1 tsp, Tahini 2 tbsp, Lemon juice 1 tbsp, Olive oil 1 tbsp, Garlic 1 clove, Salt to taste.

Method: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Adjust water for desired consistency. Drizzle olive oil and serve.

Amla Immunity Tea

A daily Ayurvedic ritual — warm water infused with Alora Amla Powder, honey and turmeric for immunity. 5 mins, 1 Cup.

Ingredients: Warm water 1 cup, Alora Amla Powder ½ tsp, Honey 1 tsp, Turmeric a pinch.

Method: Stir Amla Powder and turmeric into warm water. Add honey and mix well. Drink first thing every morning.

Iced Kashmiri Kahwa

A refreshing cold twist on the traditional Himalayan brew — saffron and cardamom over ice. 8 mins, 1 Glass.

Ingredients: Water 1 cup, Alora Kashmiri Kahwa mix 2 tsp (double strength), Honey 1 tsp, Lemon juice few drops, Ice cubes.

Method: Brew Kahwa double-strength, then let it cool. Fill a glass with ice. Pour over Kahwa, add honey and lemon. Stir and serve.

Garlic & Lemon Roasted Veggies

Wholesome roasted vegetables tossed with Alora Garlic Powder and a bright citrus lift — an easy weeknight side. 35 mins, Serves 2.

Ingredients: Mixed vegetables 2 cups, Olive oil 2 tbsp, Alora Garlic Powder 1 tbsp, Lemon juice/zest 1 lemon, Salt & pepper to taste.

Method: Toss vegetables with oil, garlic powder and seasoning. Spread on a baking tray. Roast at 200°C for 25–30 min until golden. Finish with fresh lemon juice and zest.

Black Garlic Pasta

Creamy, garlicky pasta with a rich umami kick from Alora Black Garlic Powder — restaurant-style, ready in 25 minutes. 25 mins, Serves 2.

Ingredients: Pasta 200 g, Olive oil 2 tbsp, Garlic (chopped) 1 tbsp, Alora Black Garlic Powder 1 tbsp, Cream ¼ cup, Parmesan 2 tbsp, Salt & pepper to taste.

Method: Cook pasta, reserve some starchy water. Sauté garlic, stir in Black Garlic Powder. Add cream, season, mix well. Toss in pasta with parmesan and serve.

Banana Smoothie

A creamy, naturally sweet smoothie made with Alora Banana Powder — no fresh banana needed. 5 mins, 1 Glass.

Ingredients: Milk 1 cup, Alora Banana Powder 2 tbsp, Honey 1 tsp, Chia seeds ½ tsp (optional).

Method: Add milk, Banana Powder and honey to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. Stir in chia seeds and serve chilled.

Spinach Powder Soup

A vibrant, iron-rich soup made in minutes with Alora Spinach Powder — no washing or chopping needed. 10 mins, Serves 2.

Ingredients: Alora Spinach Powder 2 tbsp, Water 400 ml, Garlic ½ tsp, Salt to taste, Cream 2 tbsp.

Method: Whisk Spinach Powder into warm water until smooth. Add garlic and salt, heat 5 minutes. Stir in cream and serve hot.

🍵 Classic Kashmiri Kahwa

A Himalayan tradition — warming, aromatic and rich in wellness

01

Add 1 tsp Alora Kahwa to 250ml hot water. Do not boil.

02

Steep 2–3 mins. Saffron releases its beautiful golden colour.

03

Strain into a cup using a fine strainer.

04

Add honey to taste. Garnish with a saffron strand. 🌸
