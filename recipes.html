How to Use Alora Products

From smoothies to soups, skincare to seasoning — discover the many ways our organic, farm fresh dehydrated products can enrich your food and life.

Category 01
Fruit Powder Uses & Recipes
Orange Peel Powder
Rich in Vitamin C · Natural Flavour · Multi-purpose
☕ Herbal Tea 🧁 Baking 🥤 Smoothies 💆 Skincare 🍫 Chocolate 🏠 Room Freshener
☕ Orange Peel Tea

Add ½ tsp orange peel powder to hot water with honey and ginger. Steep 3 mins. A refreshing Vitamin C boost in every sip.

🧁 Orange Cake

Add 1 tbsp orange peel powder to your cake batter for a natural citrus flavour without any artificial essence.

💆 Face Mask

Mix 1 tsp orange peel powder with honey and yogurt. Apply for 15 mins — natural brightening and anti-tan mask.

🥤 Citrus Smoothie

Blend ½ tsp with banana, milk and honey for a natural Vitamin C smoothie — fresh, tangy and nutritious.

Banana Powder
High in Potassium · Natural Sweetener · Baby Food Friendly
🍼 Baby Food 🥤 Smoothies 🥞 Pancakes 🍪 Baking 💪 Protein Shakes 🍨 Ice Cream
🥤 Banana Smoothie

Mix 2 tbsp banana powder with milk, honey and a pinch of cinnamon. Blend and enjoy — natural energy booster.

🥞 Banana Pancakes

Replace fresh banana with 3 tbsp banana powder in your pancake batter for consistent flavour and natural sweetness.

🍼 Baby Porridge

Stir 1 tsp banana powder into warm rice or oat porridge for babies — natural, nutritious and easy to digest.

💪 Protein Shake

Add 2 tbsp banana powder to your protein shake for natural carbohydrates and potassium — perfect post-workout.

Amla Powder
Highest Natural Vitamin C · Immunity Booster · Ayurvedic
🏥 Health Supplement ☕ Amla Tea 🥤 Juice 💆 Hair Care 🍯 Chutney 💊 Nutraceuticals
☕ Amla Immunity Tea

Mix ½ tsp amla powder with warm water, honey and a pinch of turmeric. Drink every morning for immunity boost.

💆 Hair Oil Mask

Mix 2 tbsp amla powder with coconut oil, warm gently, apply to scalp. Leave 1 hour. Natural hair strengthening treatment.

🥤 Amla Juice

Mix 1 tsp amla powder in a glass of water with honey and black salt. Rich in Vitamin C — refreshing health drink.

🥒 Amla Turmeric Pickle

Grate fresh amla (more amla than turmeric). Heat oil, add mustard seeds, hing and red chilli powder. Add amla, turmeric and salt. Mix well with pickle masala. Once cooled, store in a glass jar. Refrigerate and shake daily. Lasts up to 1 month! 🌿

🍯 Amla Chutney

Mix amla powder with green chilli, ginger and salt. A tangy condiment perfect with Indian meals and snacks.

Pomegranate Powder
Rich in Antioxidants · Natural Colour · Heart Health
🥤 Smoothies 🧁 Baking 💊 Supplements 🍹 Drinks 🍦 Desserts 💆 Skincare
🥤 Pomegranate Smoothie

Blend 1 tsp pomegranate powder with yogurt, honey and berries. Vibrant, antioxidant-rich and naturally sweet.

🍹 Health Drink

Mix 1 tsp in cold water with lemon and honey. A refreshing drink rich in polyphenols and natural antioxidants.

Category 02
Vegetable Powder Uses & Recipes

🍅 Tomato Powder Soup Recipe

Quick, rich and full of natural lycopene — made in under 10 minutes!

01

Mix 3 tbsp tomato powder with 400ml warm water. Stir until smooth.

02

Add ½ tsp garlic powder, salt, pepper and a pinch of sugar.

03

Heat in a pan for 5 mins. Add cream or butter for richness.

04

Garnish with fresh basil or croutons. Serve hot! 🍅

Category 04
Dehydrated Vegetables Uses & Recipes

🥕 Mixed Vegetable Soup Recipe

A hearty, nutritious soup using Alora dehydrated vegetables — ready in 15 minutes!

01

Soak 2 tbsp each of onion flakes, tomato and potato slices in warm water for 10 mins.

02

Sauté rehydrated veg in butter with garlic powder, salt and pepper for 3 mins.

03

Add 500ml vegetable stock and 1 tsp tomato powder. Simmer 10 mins.

04

Blend half for a creamy texture. Serve with bread and fresh herbs! 🌿

Category 05
Kashmiri Khawa Tea Uses & Brewing

🍵 Classic Kashmiri Khawa Recipe

A Himalayan tradition — warming, aromatic and rich in wellness

01

Add 1 tsp Alora Khawa to 250ml hot water. Do not boil.

02

Steep for 2-3 minutes. The saffron will release its golden colour.

03

Strain into a cup using a fine strainer.

04

Add honey or sugar to taste. Garnish with a strand of saffron. 🌸

Kashmiri Khawa Tea
Traditional · Aromatic · Wellness Tea
☕ Morning Wellness 🌙 Evening Wind-Down 🤒 Cold & Flu Relief 🧘 Meditation Tea 🎁 Gift Sets 🏨 Hospitality
🥛 Khawa Latte

Brew Khawa strong (2 tsp), strain and add steamed milk with honey. A warming, aromatic Indian chai latte twist!

🧊 Iced Khawa

Brew double strength, allow to cool, pour over ice with honey and a squeeze of lemon — refreshing summer drink!

🤒 Cold Relief Brew

Brew Khawa with extra ginger powder and honey. The saffron, cardamom and cinnamon soothe cold and flu symptoms.

🍮 Khawa Dessert

Use brewed Khawa as the liquid base for rice kheer or panna cotta — adds beautiful colour and aromatic flavour.
